# Introduction to Dr. Ju-Chun (JC) KO

![dAAAb8](/image/dAAAb8.jpg)

Born Ju-Chun Ko, he is commonly known as JC.

## Current Positions

- **Legislator-at-Large of the Kuomintang (KMT)**
- **President/Founder of the Legislative Yuan Emerging Technology Exchange Association (ETEA)**
- **Co-chair of the Legislative-Yuan USA Caucus (LUC)**
- Adjunct assistant professor at the National Taiwan University's Institute of Networking and Multimedia

## Involvement

Regarding my involvement in affairs and platforms related to pluralistic democracy, the story can start from January 2015. Jaclyn Tsai, a minister without portfolio in the Executive Yuan and the initiator of vTaiwan, [invited me and Audrey](https://www.youtube.com/live/kcA3O_6p61k?si=hinBouBNw5Sjzb6E) to teach a class for nearly a hundred senior government officials, sharing the possibilities of digital government and virtual worlds.

At that time, I was also invited to be an advisor for the Executive Yuan's Virtual World Regulatory Adjustment Project and participated in the promotion of [vTaiwan](https://vTaiwan.tw).

In November of the same year (2015), I participated in the planning and production of the 'Talk to Taiwan' online TV show episodes.

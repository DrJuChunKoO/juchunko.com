# ETech Wiki

## Metaverse

A richer, more fun gaming universe that integrates socializing, life, and work!

## Blockchain

A new network technology that allows the transfer of "value" without trust.

## Virtual World

An invisible, intangible yet three-dimensional, and lifelike world that mimics reality.

## Generative AI

Unlike past recognition-based AI, it is an artificial intelligence technology that can easily generate indistinguishable images, texts, and videos through computation.
